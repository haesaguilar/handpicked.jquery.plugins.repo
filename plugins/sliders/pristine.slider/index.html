Supported Effects

I'm extremely happy to announce that Pristine Slider supports three of the more general and popular effects for a slider, plus a unique one:

  • Slide
  • Fade
  • 2D Transforms

Compiler

PHP app for easy setup.

Pristine Slider comes with a PHP compiler, that will do the dirty work for you. You simply specify some settings like effect, width, height, timing and the compiler will generate all files for you!
